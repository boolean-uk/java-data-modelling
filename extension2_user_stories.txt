Epic

A car hire company with multiple locations wants to design an online system that will allow
customers to book various cars, on various dates from different locations. Design the database
system that would allow them to effectively manage their business.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

User Stories

    - As a customer, I want to be able to reserve a car several days in advance.

    - As a company manager, I want to be to see when a customer gets and returns a car.

    - As a company manager, I want to be able to add a location as my business grows.

    - As a customer, I want to see the available cars and locations.

    - As a company manager, I want to be able to add new cars as they are purchased.
