# User stories


```
1. 
As a customer, I want to create an account on the online store so that I can make purchases.
As a customer, I want to browse and view details of different products available in the store.
As a customer, I want to add products to my shopping cart.
As a customer, I want to review my shopping cart and proceed to checkout.
As a customer, I want to provide shipping information during the checkout process.
As a customer, I want to view the status of my orders and past purchase history.
As a store manager, I want to add, update, or remove products from the inventory.
As a store manager, I want to view customer details and order history.
As a store manager, I want to process and update the status of customer orders.
```

```
2. 
As a customer, I want to create an account on the car hire platform.
As a customer, I want to browse and view details of available cars at different locations.
As a customer, I want to select a car, specify pickup and return dates, and book it.
As a customer, I want to provide necessary information during the booking process.
As a customer, I want to view my booking details and history.
As a company, I want to manage a fleet of cars at different locations.
As a company, I want to track car availability and update it based on bookings.
As a company, I want to view and manage customer bookings.
```


```
3. 
As a student, I want to create an account on the library system.
As a user, I want to browse and search for available books and other items.
As a user, I want to check the availability of a specific book.
As a user, I want to borrow up to 6 items at a time.
As a user, I want to view my borrowing history and due dates.
As a librarian, I want to manage the library inventory.
As a librarian, I want to update item availability based on borrows and returns.
```
